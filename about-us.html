About Us

We are not just another vehicle grooming business. We turn the clock back on your ride.

At Luxury Auto Detailing, we pride ourselves on offering the best detailing services in Auckland. We take vehicle detailing to another level with our attention to detail and industry-leading techniques. We also use the best materials and equipment available on the market.

This isn’t just a job for us, as we are passionate about vehicles and making them look their best. You can also expect excellent customer service as we want to build a long-lasting relationship where you continue to contact us whenever you need detailing services for your vehicle.

Fully Bespoke – And We Come to You

All the services we offer are fully bespoke. You will find a guide and information on our services here on this website, but we’ll put together a package that suits your requirements and your vehicle. Furthermore, our services are mobile, so we’ll come to you. We operate throughout the Auckland area.

Continuous Improvement

Our team at Luxury Auto Detailing receives regular training and we stay on top of the latest developments in our industry. It is an industry that doesn’t stand still, and we are committed to continuous improvement. We are also committed to doing our part for the environment, so we take into account the impact of our services, putting mitigation measures in place wherever possible.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to serve our customers and always deliver the highest level of customer service; to develop our team and strive to constantly improve; and to conduct ourselves in an environmentally responsible manner.

Our Vision

To be the world’s most exciting leader in automotive business intelligence solutions. We will generate excitement through implementing pioneering ideas, problem solving & going beyond our customers’ expectations.

Core Values

  • Teamwork through Trust and Respect.
  • Commitment to Customer Enthusiasm.
  • Accountability at all Levels.
  • Passion for Winning.
